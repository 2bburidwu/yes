Don Quixote

A Classic Novel by Miguel de Cervantes

About the Book

Don Quixote is a Spanish novel that tells the story of a man who becomes a knight-errant after reading too many chivalric romances. He sets out on adventures with his loyal squire, Sancho Panza.

Main Themes

  • Reality vs Illusion
  • Idealism
  • Adventure and Honor
